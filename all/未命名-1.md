```mermaid
sequenceDiagram
    participant Employee
    participant System
    participant Database
    participant HR
    participant IncentiveDB

    Employee->>+System: Register for program
    System->>+Database: Store registration data
    Database-->>-System: Registration confirmed

    System->>+HR: Notify about new registration
    HR-->>-System: Acknowledge registration

    loop Weekly Participation Reminder
        System->>+Employee: Send reminder
        Employee-->>-System: Log participation
    end

    System->>+IncentiveDB: Check for incentive eligibility
    alt Eligible for Incentive
        IncentiveDB-->>-System: Eligible for incentive
        System->>+HR: Notify to offer incentive
        HR-->>-Employee: Incentive offered
    else Not Eligible
        IncentiveDB-->>-System: Not eligible for incentive
        System-->>-Employee: No incentive awarded
    end
    deactivate IncentiveDB
```
